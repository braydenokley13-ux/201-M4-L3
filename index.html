üìã What's a GM?

A General Manager (GM) is the person who builds the team's roster. They make trades, sign free agents, and pick players in the draft. Think of them as the team's architect!

The Three Team Types

üî® REBUILDING TEAMS (4+ year plan)

Example: San Antonio Spurs after trading away their veterans

Goal: Collect young players and draft picks for the future

Best moves: Keep picks, trade down for more picks, develop young talent

Worst moves: Trading for expensive veterans who'll be old when you're good

‚úÖ OKC collected tons of draft picks while rebuilding ‚Äî now they have Shai Gilgeous-Alexander + a great young core!

üìà RISING TEAMS (2 year window)

Example: OKC Thunder with their young superstar core

Goal: Add pieces that help you compete soon without giving up the future

Best moves: Keep picks or make balanced trades for players ready to contribute

Worst moves: Being too aggressive OR too patient ‚Äî find the balance!

‚úÖ The Knicks traded for OG Anunoby at the right time ‚Äî he fit their rising timeline perfectly!

üèÜ WIN NOW TEAMS (Championship window is NOW)

Example: Boston Celtics who just won the championship

Goal: Win a championship THIS YEAR ‚Äî future picks don't matter as much

Best moves: Trade picks for veterans who can help you win immediately

Worst moves: Keeping picks (rookies rarely help championship teams)

‚úÖ Boston traded a 1st round pick for Derrick White ‚Äî he helped them win the championship!

Understanding Trade Types

‚¨ÜÔ∏è Trade Up

Move to an earlier pick (costs extra assets). You get a better chance at a star, but you pay picks/players to do it.

‚¨áÔ∏è Trade Down

Move to a later pick (gain extra assets). You get more picks total, but each pick is less valuable.

üëî Trade for Veteran

Exchange pick for a proven NBA player. You know what you're getting, but the player might not fit your timeline.

‚≠ê Swing for a Star

Go big! Trade lots of assets for a star player. High reward potential, but RISKY if it doesn't work out.

‚ùå Brooklyn traded ALL their picks for aging stars... then the stars got hurt and left. Now they have nothing!
